# IPL 2025 Data Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the IPL 2025 season using match-level, ball-by-ball, and player performance data. The goal is to present the season in a simple, business-friendly way so that team performance, venue patterns, toss impact, and player contributions can be understood clearly by decision makers. 

## Executive Summary
- **Ultimate Champion:** RCB secured 11 match wins. 
- **Batting Powerhouse:** SRH scored 2,950 total runs.
- **Bowling Dominance:** GT claimed 95 total wickets. 
- **Standout Players:** Sai Sudharsan scored 759 runs, and Prasidh Krishna took 25 wickets. 

## Problem Statement
The 2025 Indian Premier League dataset contains match-level, ball-by-ball, and player performance data. The objective of this project is to analyze the season in a simple and professional way so that overall team performance, venue trends, toss impact, and player contributions can be clearly explained. 

## Objectives
- Identify the most successful teams in IPL 2025 based on match wins, batting output, and bowling performance. 
- Study whether toss decisions and toss results had any meaningful influence on the final match outcome. 
- Compare batting-friendly and bowling-friendly venues using match scores and venue frequency. 
- Find the top-performing batters and bowlers using the Orange Cap and Purple Cap datasets. 
- Highlight high-impact players, teams, and venues in a format that can be easily explained to higher management. 

## Key Questions Answered
- Which team won the most matches in the 2025 season? 
- Did teams prefer to bowl first after winning the toss? 
- How often did the toss winner also win the match? 
- Which venues produced the highest scoring matches? 
- Which batters scored the most runs and which bowlers took the most wickets? 
- Which teams showed the strongest overall balance between batting and bowling? 

## Dataset Used
- `matches.csv` — match result, venue, team, toss, score, and award details. 
- `deliveries.csv` — ball-by-ball batting and bowling performance. 
- `orange_cap.csv` — top batters table. 
- `purple_cap.csv` — top bowlers table. 

## Important Season Insights
- RCB dominated the season with 11 wins, and the season had 74 total matches, with 73 decided and 1 tied. 
- The toss winner also won the match in 54.05% of cases, showing a moderate toss advantage. 
- Teams showed a strong preference to bowl first after winning the toss. 
- Narendra Modi Stadium and Eden Gardens hosted the highest number of matches. 
- Rajiv Gandhi International Stadium and Arun Jaitley Stadium were among the highest-scoring venues. 
- Ishan Kishan received the most Player of the Match awards, indicating high impact in decisive moments. 

## Expected Outcome
The final output includes clear charts, tables, and short insight statements that can be used directly in a portfolio presentation. The analysis helps explain season trends, player impact, and team performance in a simple and professional format.
